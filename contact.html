Alright! You're only one step away from getting *That Sound*.

Use the form below to get in touch with me, let me know about your project and ask any questions you might have.
The form below is disabled in this demo, feel free to contact me directly at ivan.chepurin@gmail.com
Here's what will happen after you contact me:
  • You will recieve an auto-reply message with some followup info (If not, check your spam folder);
  • I will respond to you as soon as I see your request. We will discuss your idea, maybe you will provide a rough mix alongside with the titles of the songs that you want me to use as references;
  • You will send me the files;
  • I will quickly check them out, and if everything is ok, I'll send you the invoice and, right after you've paid it, start working on your music;
  • At this point you can just chill for the duration of specified turnaround time, while I work my studio magic on your tracks;
  • Once I decide that I've done everything to make your music sound the best it can, I'll let you know and send you the .mp3 of your song;
  • You'll have a listen. If you love it and you're 100% satisfied – awesome, I will deliver the .WAV file. If you have a different idea about how certain part or instrument should sound, you'll let me know, and I will go back and change it to your liking. Then we'll repeat from the previous step
  • Then we'll call it a wrap. You'll be free to do anything you want with your awesome-sounding music, and I'll be waiting for you to return to my studio with some more newly recorded songs.
