SERVICES & RATES
MIX
150$
Mixing is a fundamental part of music production process. Groovy, punchy, crisp, dynamic, energetic, big, fat, in-your-face, jumping out of the speakers – all these qualities of a song are determined by its mix.

What I'll need: all of your song's instruments, track by track (WAV/AIFF), starting at 00:00. You should not apply any processing to the tracks. If you have a rough mix, send it over as well.

What you'll get: your music mixed with love and precision, ready to be mastered. If you aren't 100% satisfied with the result, you can let me know and we'll do the revision(s).

Turnaround: ~3 days
MASTER
70$
Mastering is the final step of music production. In this stage, audio engineer makes sure that your music not only sounds the best it can on as many sound systems as possible, but is also ready for distribution.
If you had someone else mixing your song, or you mixed it by yourself, you can choose me to master it for you.

What I'll need: just the stereo mix (WAV/AIFF). Stem mastering is also an option.

What you'll get: your music mastered to industry standard quality with attention to detail, ready to be uploaded to the platform of your choosing.

Turnaround: 1-2 days
ORDER SERVICES

You may be wondering why I don't offer mixing with mastering included. That's because I recommend you to consider hiring two different engineers for these two different jobs. Mastering isn't just about getting that commercial loudness. First of all it's a second opinion, second set of ears to critically listen for what requires final touches. However, I leave you the right to disagree and ignore my advice. If you insist, I will both mix and master your song.
